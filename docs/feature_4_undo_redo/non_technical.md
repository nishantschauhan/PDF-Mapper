# Product Overview: Undo & Redo System

## What it does
The Undo/Redo feature acts as a safety net for the user. If they accidentally delete a signature box, place a variable on the wrong page, or make a typo in a variable's name, they can easily step backward in time to fix it, just like they would in Microsoft Word or Google Docs.

## User Benefits

* **Total Forgiveness:** Users are free to experiment with their document layout without the fear of permanently breaking something. Every single action—adding, moving, renaming, or deleting—can be reversed with a single click or keystroke.

* **Familiar Shortcuts:** The system natively supports standard keyboard shortcuts (`Ctrl+Z` to Undo, `Ctrl+Y` to Redo) so users don't even have to lift their hands off the keyboard or search for UI buttons to fix a mistake.

* **Granular Control:** The system remembers exactly where a variable was placed before it was moved. If a user spends time perfectly aligning a box and then accidentally bumps it with their mouse, hitting Undo snaps it right back to its perfect, original location.

## Business Value
A highly forgiving user interface drastically reduces user frustration and support tickets. When users know they can safely recover from their own mistakes, they build templates faster, abandon the software less frequently, and report higher overall satisfaction with the product's UX.
